It's finally here, and I am so thrilled to share it with you!

**One Puppy, Two Puppies, Hey What Do You Do?** is now available as a paperback on Amazon, and I honestly can't stop smiling about it. This little book has been such a joy to create, and I hope it brings just as much joy into your home as it has into mine.

## What Is It All About?

At its heart, this is a counting book - but it's so much more than just numbers on a page.

We count from one to ten alongside the most adorable, mischievous puppies you've ever seen. Each number brings a new activity: puppies running through sunshine, digging in the garden, splashing in puddles, tugging on a rope, and jumping for treats. And at every turn, the book invites your little one to join in: *"Come do it too!"*

The rhyming text bounces along with a rhythm that toddlers naturally love. It's the kind of book that gets read again and again (and again!) - and I designed it that way on purpose. Repetition is how little ones learn, and when learning feels like play, magic happens.

## What Makes This Book Special

There are a few things I'm particularly proud of with this one.

**The animated number characters.** Each number from 1 to 10 has its own personality and appears throughout the book as a little character. They wave, they dance, they cheer the puppies on. Children notice them, point at them, and start to recognise the numbers as friendly faces rather than abstract symbols. It's a small touch that makes a real difference.

**The diversity of dog breeds.** I wanted every page to feel like a celebration of all the wonderful dogs out there. You'll spot golden retrievers, dalmatians, beagles, pugs, poodles, dachshunds, corgis, and more. Little dog lovers will have a wonderful time spotting and naming the different breeds - it's a whole extra layer of engagement beyond the counting.

**The bedtime ending.** After all that running and jumping and splashing, the book winds down to the most tender moment: a child cuddling up with their one special puppy at bedtime. *"But I love just one, my one and only pup, to cuddle before sleep... we snuggle right up!"* It's the perfect way to transition from playtime energy to bedtime calm, and I won't lie - it gets me every time.

## Who Is This Book For?

I wrote **One Puppy, Two Puppies** for toddlers and preschoolers aged 0-4, but truly, it's for anyone who loves puppies and believes that learning should feel like an adventure.

- **Parents** looking for a bedtime book that teaches counting without feeling like a lesson
- **Educators** wanting an interactive read-aloud that gets children moving and participating
- **Grandparents and gift-givers** searching for that perfect present for a puppy-loving little one
- **Dog-obsessed families** who want a book that celebrates the bond between children and their pets

The interactive prompts make it wonderful for group reading too - imagine a room full of toddlers pretending to splash in puddles or jump for treats!
