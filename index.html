"One Puppy, Two Puppies, Hey What Do You Do?"

by Rebecca Mola

Does your little one love puppies and learning to count? This delightful book combines both in the most heartwarming way!

Join ten playful puppies as they invite your child into their world of fun activities. From running in sunshine to digging in gardens, splashing in puddles to jumping for treats, each page brings new adventures and counting practice.

This sweet story celebrates both learning and the precious relationship between children and their beloved pets. Every page invites little ones to join in the fun while building essential early Maths skills.

Perfect for bedtime cuddles, this book will have you and your child snuggling up together, just like the child and puppy in the story!

Meet the Author

Rebecca Mola

Rebecca is dedicated to creating engaging children's literature that combines learning with joy, and believes that every child deserves stories that make them laugh, learn, dream, and imagine.

Rebecca enjoys writing children's stories in her spare time as a creative outlet. With a love for learning, laughter, and imagination, she hopes her stories bring joy to young readers - whether they spark a giggle, teach a new fact, or inspire a big daydream. As a debut author, she's thrilled to share her children's books with others.

"Every child has a wild imagination waiting to be nurtured. My goal is to plant seeds of wonder that grow into lifelong love of reading and creativity." - Rebecca Mola

Our Mission

Like the books growing on our magical tree, we believe every story has the power to flourish in a child's imagination. Wild Imagination Press is dedicated to creating books that:

  • Inspire children to imagine and dream big
